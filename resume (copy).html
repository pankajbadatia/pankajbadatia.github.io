Thesis Title: Crowd Counting and Density Estimation using Multicolumn Discriminator in Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)

Project Guide: Dr. Pankaj Tasgaonkar (IIT Roorkee)

Abstract : Crowd count and Density Estimation has been done with myriads of techniques. The Multicolumn Discriminator can be applied across various disciplines such as cell counting, density of haemorrhage in eye, Blood vessel density, vehicle counting. The current state of art is improvement in Discriminator model in supervised learning using Generative Adversarial network (GAN's). In this paper we provide how Multicolumn Discriminator model can effectively improve accuracy. The dataset used in this experiment was `World Expo 10 Dataset'. Our method out performs existing GAN based method and other state of art. Furthermore this model can be used for crowd count and Density estimation.

Publisher : IEEE - International Conference on Advances in Computing, Communications and Informatics (ICACCI) - 2018. Click here to read the paper
