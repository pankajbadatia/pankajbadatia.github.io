Journey So Far.....

Embarking on my tech journey back before 2010 in India, during my engineering days, the digital landscape was a far cry from the high-speed connectivity we enjoy today. The era of 2G prevailed, and my primary source of knowledge was the classic method – diving deep into books. As the technology winds of change blew, 2G evolved into edge services, and the introduction of 3G marked a significant shift.


I vividly recall the early days of exploring YouTube with its continuous windows of buffering. Learning from those moments of patience laid the groundwork for my understanding of the evolving digital realm. Fast forward to 4G, and now the anticipation of 5G, the internet landscape has transformed. From grappling with buffering issues on YouTube to seamlessly streaming videos on data science, computer vision, and NLP, the learning curve has been nothing short of exhilarating.


Reflecting on programming in 2017, I reminisce about working with GPUs boasting just a handful of cores. Today, the landscape has evolved, with the convenience of embedded GPUs in laptops and the luxury of server accounts for more intensive tasks. In the realm of web development, my journey began in 2010 with the creation of journal websites like www.ijpijournals.com and crestjournals.com. Back then, my toolkit consisted of PHP, HTML, and CSS.


Fast forward to the present, where hosting an AI model on servers is just another milestone in a series of technological advancements. Through the years, I've not only earned valuable experience but also kept myself abreast of the ever-evolving tech landscape. The transition from 2G to 5G, from primitive buffering to seamless streaming, and from modest GPUs to advanced embedded systems – it's been a journey of continual learning and adaptation, shaping me into a tech enthusiast who thrives on staying updated in this dynamic digital era.
